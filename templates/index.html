What changed in this improved version?

  • Replaced the older baseline classifier with a stronger `LinearSVC` URL classifier.
  • Combined character-level and token-level TF-IDF features to catch spoofed brand names, fake paths, and malicious URL patterns.
  • Added a confidence score and risk hints so the prediction feels easier to explain during your project demo.
  • Refined the interface for a cleaner, more modern security-focused presentation on desktop and mobile.
